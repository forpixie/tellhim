For Pixie

The abyssal chime
Of my long lost interlude
How out of depth I am
Within this altitude

But to wreathe me and collide
In my olive alcove
Becoming a landslide
At your simplest move

Summer evening carousel
The flavour of midnight’s touch
A taste of vanilla and caramel
And a mild smell of hazelnut

A connection tethered twofold
With our story to be told
Bringing kingdoms to their knees
Embracing in sunlight’s beams
